About

honmaple is a fusion of "hon" and "maple".

"Hon" derives from honker—a term widely used in the early "Honker" (Chinese patriotic hacker) community—representing a passion for technology and an unwavering commitment to justice. "Maple" signifies the maple tree; it is an image I have long favored, symbolizing colors that are vivid and passionate yet serene.

Together, they form "honmaple". My personal blog, Song of the honmaple, draws its name from this very concept: I aspire to use words as my brush and code as my ink—like the red maple, singing my own song amidst the passage of time, while chronicling the landscapes of technology, life, and reflection.
